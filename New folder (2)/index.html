About Her

Mother Teresa, born in 1910 in Macedonia, devoted her life to serving the poor and sick in India. She founded the Missionaries of Charity, an organization that runs hospices, soup kitchens, and schools. She received the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979 and is remembered as a symbol of selfless service.

Admirable Qualities

  • Compassionate and kind-hearted
  • Dedicated to serving the poor
  • Humble and selfless
  • Strong faith and inner strength
  • A true role model for humanity
